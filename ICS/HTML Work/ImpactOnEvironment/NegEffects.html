Dangerous chemicals (Lead, Mercury, Flame retardants, Beryllium, Cadmium, etc.) Some cases of depression and insanity have been reported when the user is unable to remove themself from the "virtual world" and not being able to put down the device
Lead can be found in the glass of the monitor and the solder in the circuit board of the CPU. Lead poisoning can happen through fumes, food contamination, skin absorption. As a heavy metal it interferes with development of the nervous system. Physical effects may include carpol tunnel from using a mouse and keyboard for too long
Mercury is another heavy metal, found on circuit boards. It can cause symptoms such as burning skin, skin discoloration, peeling skin and brain/kidney damage. Loss of sleep hinders brain activity and neural development as well as hormonal growth.
Cadmium is a coating sometimes found on the glass of a monitor, in bataries, resistors and wires. It can damage kidneys and bones In extreme cases there have even been deaths from extensive use of technology.
If devices are not properly discarded it causes water or air contamination. Computer radiation can cause cancer, tumors , miscarriage and birth defects
Waste is sent to developing countries where they harvest precious metals by burning the components thus releasing toxins into the air. They seep into the water and ruin the aquatic ecosystem. Listening to music through headphones, may cause temporary hearing loss. In the worst cases, it may be permanant.
